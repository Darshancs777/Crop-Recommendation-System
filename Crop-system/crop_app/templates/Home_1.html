Welcome to the Crop Recommendation System

We are here to provide suggestions for your crop cultivation to help you achieve better yields.

We recommend the best crops for you to grow based on your soil nutrients. You can test your soil in a lab to get the necessary information.

How It Works

Our Crop Recommendation System uses advanced algorithms to analyze your soil's nutrient levels and environmental conditions. By entering your soil test results, our system can predict the most suitable crops for your farm. This helps in optimizing your yield and ensuring sustainable farming practices.

Benefits

  • Maximize crop yield
  • Ensure soil health and sustainability
  • Reduce costs and increase profitability
  • Make informed decisions based on scientific data
